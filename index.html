How it works

Membership to this community is by invitation only. Each community member is vetted by a member who knows her personally in real life.

Members then form their own networks based on physical proximity and shared connections. Friend requests are sent and accepted, thereby building each user’s individual network. When a member needs babysitting services, she submits a request for services to her network that is displayed on her friends’ pages. The requests indicate names of children and date/time babysitting assistance is needed.

Each member earns credits that can be used towards babysitting services, either by paying cash or by providing tutoring or other valuable services to members of our community This approach ensures that all members can have access to childcare services through our community, without regard to income level.
